Controls

  • Move

    You can use the [WASD] keys to move around the game arena

  • Look

    You can move the mouse to look around the game arena. When you first click on the game screen you might be prompted to confirm that you wish to hide the mouse cursor. Click yes (or whatever the confimation choice is). To get your cursor back you can press the escape key. Alternatively, you can use the arrow keys to look around the arena.

  • Shoot

    You can hold down either the mouse button or space bar to fire off a stream of projectiles.

  • Time Warp

    Holding down the shift key will cause time to slow down. This is useful when you fnd yourself surrounded by enemies and need to plan an escape route.
