SHATTER

PLAY GAME

Shatter is a fast-paced 3D arcade game that plays like a cross between Robotron: 2084 and Doom with a visual style inspired by the likes of Battlezone and Spectre.

How To Play

Gameplay

In Shatter the goal is to stay alive for as long as possible. You have a limited amount of energy (shown at the top of the screen) and when it runs out it's GAME OVER. To keep things interesting, your energy will constantly drain as you play.

To make matters worse you will also have to deal with waves of enemies trying to run into you. Every time you collide with an enemy you lose some of your precious energy. Fortunately, you can gain energy back by shooting down enemies before they reach you.

If you feel overwhelmed you can use your special Time Warp ability to slow down time and give yourself a chance to escape.

Controls

Move

You can use the [WASD] keys to move around the game arena

Look

You can move the mouse to look around the game arena. When you first click on the game screen you might be prompted to confirm that you wish to hide the mouse cursor. Click yes (or whatever your browser's confimation choice is). To get your cursor back you can press the escape key. Alternatively, you can use the arrow keys to look around the arena.

Shoot

You can hold down either the mouse button or space bar to fire off a stream of projectiles.

Time Warp

Holding down the shift key will cause time to slow down. This is useful when you fnd yourself surrounded by enemies and need to plan an escape route.
